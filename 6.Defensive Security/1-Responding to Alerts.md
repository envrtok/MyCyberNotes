Alerts are triaging by 4 key factors:
- **Severity Level**: How critical and urgency?
- **Timestamp and Frequency**: When and How Often?
- **Attack Stage**: Which stage in attack lifecycle, how far to target objective?
- **Affected Asset**: Who or what is affected?

Alert identifying by this factors and if it is critic, action steps should be started:
1) Check alert's data detailed
2) Check related logs.
3) Correlate multiple alerts.
4) Build context and a timeline.
5) Decide on an action.
6) Document findings and lessons learned.
